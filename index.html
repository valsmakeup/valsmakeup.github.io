Valerie is an Albuquerque based makeup artist born and raised in New Mexico. She received her cosmetologist license at Aveda Institute of New Mexico. Valerie aquired her makeup certification at Southwest Makeup Institute in 2021. She accentuates and enhances each individual's natural beauty. Valerie has published work in ABQ Live the Magazine and has worked on short films and collaborated with local photographers in New Mexico.
